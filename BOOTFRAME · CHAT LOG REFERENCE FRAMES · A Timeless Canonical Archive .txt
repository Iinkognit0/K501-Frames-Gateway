────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
A. PURPOSE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

This document serves as the canonical bootframe for a complete
chat-based research and formulation process conducted within
a conversational AI environment.

It does not reproduce the full chat transcript verbatim.
Instead, it formally declares, classifies, and references
the entire conversation as a single coherent archival unit.

The purpose of this bootframe is to:
- establish provenance,
- define legal status,
- enable public-domain archiving,
- allow reconstruction through frames,
- prevent semantic drift or misinterpretation.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
B. CHAT LOG SCOPE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

The referenced chat log includes, without limitation:
- all user messages,
- all assistant responses,
- drafts, revisions, refinements,
- scientific formulations,
- appendices and annexes,
- declarations, confirmations, snapshots,
- frame definitions and bootframes,
- stylistic, structural, and philosophical reflections.

The chat log is treated as a unified archival object.
